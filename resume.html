Ralph Allen, AIA

Profile

Ralph has over 40 years of experience leading complex teams to successful outcomes across major market sectors, including healthcare, commercial, hospitality, and residential. He brings strong design fundamentals, technical insight into component performance, and clear communication with clients and project teams.

  • 1.4M+ SF of highly technical hospital and healthcare facilities

Relevant Experience

Harborview Bond Issue — MIMP Revision & Misc. Project Planning

Led TGB Architects’ team in finalizing revisions to the MIMP currently in public comment; developed associated planning studies for parking and new behavioral health structures within the MIO boundary and on adjacent properties.

Western State Hospital

Led planning and design of a new TRC unit within the existing campus; served as project manager for an extensive anti-ligature renovation of several wards, and a fire door replacement project.

Project HOPE — Shanghai Children’s Medical Center (Shanghai, China)

Led the consulting team during planning and development of the Shanghai Children’s Medical Center—described as the first tertiary-care pediatric teaching facility built to modern hospital standards in Asia. (Senior Associate, NBBJ)

Two Union Square — Plaza Replacement & Re-design (Seattle, WA) | $10M | 40,000 SF

Project Manager and Project Architect for a full plaza replacement and re-design in downtown Seattle: an acre of public plaza space across three levels over occupied spaces below, including new refined design elements.

1101 Westlake — Main Lobby Renovations

Led the TGB team for a renovated main lobby intended to create an elegant, inviting arrival experience for tenants and guests, in partnership with Turner Construction.

Rural Health Care Clinic — Surmang Foundation (Tibet) | 5,000 SF

Board member and designer for a new rural health care clinic serving a remote indigenous population in a high valley in Tibet.

Selected Project Stories

  1. 9th & Alder Behavioral Health Tower (concept) — Concept solution to maximize the available envelope while satisfying the BH program and additional Harborview functions. Managed building core planning and developed Revit modeling (TGB Architects).
  2. TRC Unit — Western State Hospital — Initial designs used corridor transition points to emphasize staff connectivity and a strong sense of arrival at a patient-centered amenity. Led planning/design; elements later re-considered during value engineering.
  3. Shanghai Children’s Medical Center — Began as a 250-bed greenfield hospital with modern surgical suite, diagnostics, and MEPC systems; completed in under three years. Led NBBJ consulting team supporting Project HOPE and coordination with a major Chinese AE firm.
  4. Two Union Square — Public Plaza Waterproofing — One-acre plaza over multiple levels. Added new stairways, paving patterns, and canopies to enhance tenant/public experience (Evolution Architecture).
  5. 1101 Westlake — Lobby Renovation — Opportunity to execute a small project to a very high level of design aspiration and detail; delivered a standout finished space with an engaged owner and TGB/Turner team.
  6. Surmang Foundation Clinic — Scaled clinic design to a two-exam-room facility supporting two Tibetan doctors. Care is free; clinic sees 20,000+ patient visits annually; ongoing fundraising for capital and operations.
