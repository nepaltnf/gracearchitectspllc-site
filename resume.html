Ralph Allen, AIA

Ralph has over 40 years of experience in a vast array of project types, sizes and configurations here and around the world. His extensive background in leading complex teams to successful outcomes spans all major market sectors including healthcare, commercial, hospitality, and residential. He approaches all of his work with a strong grasp of design principles, technical insight into component performance and clear and effective communications with the client and the entire project team.

Over his career, Ralph has led the design, documentation, permitting and successful completion of

  • 1.2 million SF of commercial tenant improvement space
  • $300m+ worth of building envelope expert review, associated repair, and reconstruction projects
  • Multiple planning studies for hospitals, multi-family, and commercial developments
  • 1.4 million+ SF of highly technical hospital and health care facilities

Relevant experience

Harborview Bond Issue – MIMP Revision & Planning

Ralph led the TGB Architect’s team in finalizing revisions to the MIMP currently in public comment, developing associated planning studies for parking and new behavior health structures.

Western State Hospital

Ralph led planning and design of the new TRC unit and served as project manager for anti-ligature renovations and fire door replacement projects.

Shanghai Children’s Medical Center, China

Ralph led the consulting team during development of Asia’s first modern tertiary pediatric teaching hospital. Completed in under three years.

Two Union Square, Seattle

Project Manager and Architect for a full plaza replacement and re-design covering one acre of public space over occupied areas.

1101 Westlake – Main Lobby Renovation

Led the design team in creating a refined, elegant lobby environment within BioMed Realty’s portfolio.

Surmang Foundation Clinic, Tibet

Board member and designer for a rural health clinic serving remote villages with over 20,000 annual visits.
