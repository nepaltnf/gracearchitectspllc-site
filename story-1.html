9th & Alder

The 9th&Alder Behavioral Health Tower is a concept study designed to maximize the site’s allowable envelope while accommodating the Behavioral Health program and additional Harborview Hospital functions. A functional architectural concept arose, allowing for a large south facing roof deck available to patient and staff alike.

Ralph led the building core planning and developed the project’s Revit model while at TGB Architects.
