Two Union Square

The public plaza waterproofing project at Two Union Square encompassed a one-acre, multi-level plaza in the heart of downtown Seattle. Under the owner’s direction, new stairways, paving patterns, and canopy structures were introduced to elevate both the tenant and public experience within this landmark civic space. Ralph led the planning and design of the project while at Evolution Architecture.
