733 Lake and Harbor Lights

Two sibling buildings in Kirkland sit across a shared park, facing the water — a pairing that gives the project its distinctive character. 733 Lake and Harbor Lights involved a complete exterior renovation of both structures: full strip and reclad with materials selected for longevity, targeting a 50-year-plus lifespan while delivering a cohesive aesthetic upgrade across the pair.
