Surmang Clinic

The Surmang Foundation has been a long-standing commitment of Ralph’s. The current clinic design refines earlier full-campus concepts into a focused two-exam-room facility supporting the two Tibetan physicians on staff. Care is provided free of charge, and the clinic serves a broad catchment area of rural villages, accommodating more than 20,000 patient visits annually. Ongoing efforts support both construction of the new facility and continued operational funding.
