Western State TRC

The initial design for the Treatment and Recovery Center at Western State Hospital leveraged corridor transition points to strengthen both staff connectivity to the patient population at one end and a clear sense of arrival at a patient-centered amenity at the other. The detail plan illustrates the latter condition, developed to support a dignified and high-quality patient experience. This element is currently under review as part of a value engineering effort.

Ralph serves as the lead planner and designer for the project.
